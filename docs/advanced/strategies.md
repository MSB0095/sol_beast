# Trading Strategies

Learn effective strategies for using SOL BEAST.

## Strategy Types

### Scalping
Quick profit-taking with high frequency:
- **Goal**: Small gains (5-15%)
- **Hold Time**: 1-3 minutes
- **Risk**: Low per trade
- **Volume**: High

### Swing Trading
Medium-term holds:
- **Goal**: Moderate gains (30-100%)
- **Hold Time**: 5-15 minutes
- **Risk**: Moderate
- **Volume**: Medium

### Position Trading
Longer holds for bigger gains:
- **Goal**: Large gains (100-500%)
- **Hold Time**: 15-60 minutes
- **Risk**: Higher
- **Volume**: Lower

## Best Practices

1. **Diversify**: Don't put all capital in one token
2. **Start Small**: Test strategies with minimal capital
3. **Monitor**: Watch trades actively
4. **Adjust**: Refine based on performance
5. **Risk Management**: Never risk more than 1-2% per trade
