# Color Themes

SOL BEAST features 7 cyberpunk-inspired color themes.

## Available Themes

| Theme | ID | Primary Color | Style |
|-------|-----|--------------|--------|
| MATRIX | sol-green | `#00ff41` | Classic green |
| NEON | emerald | `#10ffb0` | Vibrant emerald |
| CYBER | blue | `#00d9ff` | Cool cyan |
| PLASMA | purple | `#d946ef` | Electric purple |
| LASER | rose | `#ff0062` | Hot pink |
| GOLD | amber | `#ffb000` | Warm amber |
| TRON | cyan | `#00ffff` | Pure cyan |

## Switching Themes

Click the THEME button in the top-right corner of the dashboard or docs.

## Persistence

Your theme preference is saved to localStorage and persists across sessions.
